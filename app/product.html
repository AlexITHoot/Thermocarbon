Why Dicemaster dicing blades

Highest Quality Abrasives

Dicemaster® is composed of the highest quality diamond abrasives, from its inner to the outer diameter. They are evenly dispersed to ensure highquality cuts in the most difficult materials. An almost infinite variety of diamond/filler combinations enables multiple performance capabilities to fit various application requirements.

Self-Sharpening

Your technicians will appreciate Dicemaster’s remarkable self-sharpening characteristics, which eliminate the need for dressing procedures and costly interruptions to production

Personalized Service

In order to specifically address your individual needs, our experienced staff will help you to choose from among our Dicemaster® and Blademaster® blades. Additionally, our R&D lab, which is one of the most sophisticated in the country will custom formulate the blade matrix to best meet your most demanding requirements.

High Yields

Thermocarbon’s unequaled production and quality control standards ensure receiving consistent performance from blade to blade. The result is higher yields, faster feed rates, and increased productivity.
